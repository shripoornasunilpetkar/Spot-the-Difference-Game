Spot the Difference

Welcome to Spot the Difference!

This game challenges you to find the differences between two nearly identical images. Test your observation skills and see how quickly you can spot all the differences!

How to Play:

  1. Two images will be displayed side by side
  2. Look carefully at both images to find the differences
  3. Click on the difference in the LEFT image only
  4. When you find a difference, it will be highlighted with a red border
  5. The game tracks your time and score
  6. Find all differences to complete the game!
